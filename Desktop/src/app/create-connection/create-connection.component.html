Connection Instructions

IMPORTANT: Both this app and your mobile device MUST be connected to the same network.

  1. 1 Install Traccar Client app on your mobile device
  2. 2 Open app and turn OFF the service status
  3. 3 Set Location Accuracy to HIGH in device settings
  4. 4 Tap "Server URL" and enter: http://{{ serverIp }}:{{ serverPort }}
  5. 5 Set Frequency to 1 second and save settings
  6. 6 Turn ON the service status to start tracking
